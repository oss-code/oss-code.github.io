10 * \brief The old deprecated Cpuset API.
11 * This interface should not be used anymore, it will be dropped in a later release.
12 *
13 * hwloc/bitmap.h should be used instead. Most hwloc_cpuset_foo functions are
14 * replaced with hwloc_bitmap_foo. The only exceptions are:
15 * - hwloc_cpuset_from_string -> hwloc_bitmap_sscanf
16 * - hwloc_cpuset_cpu -> hwloc_bitmap_only
17 * - hwloc_cpuset_all_but_cpu -> hwloc_bitmap_allbut
18 */
